Service and Resource About Us

The Wabash Media Center is the place where campus media contents are produce professionally by our student wokers. We produce media material for Wabash such as posters, podcasts, promotional videos, and live broadcastings. We also provide service in printing, laminating, as well as media producing and editing.
It is aslo a dynamic workplace for students and faculties. We have 14 iMac workstations, all of which has software for photo, audio, and video editing.
Contact us at: 765-361-6451 (operator desk) and 765-361-6261 (Adam Phipps - MC Director)
You can follow our work via Facebook and Twitter or visit the recomended pages for inspiration.
